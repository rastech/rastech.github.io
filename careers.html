About Us

Meta allows you to seamlessly search for, access, and share files across all cloud platforms and devices. Our search engine learns and improves itself as you interact with it, leveraging natural language processing and automatic tagging to provide accurate results every single time.

We just closed an early stage seed round and are looking to add to our core team. We're in Boston, two blocks away from South Station. Apply now for ground floor entry to an exciting project that is supported by prominent investors and enthusiastic early adopters. More importantly, you'll have a huge impact this early on. We can provide compensation commensurate with experience and there are ground floor stock options available!

OSX Engineer

You write efficient, elegant, and testable Objective-C and Swift to a high standard, in a timely and scalable way. You’re familiar with CoreData, AFNetworking, File System Events, XPC Services, Background/Login Applications, Status Bar Items, Spotlight Integrations, Local and Remote Notifications, App Sandbox, and Accessibility requirements. Intimate familiarity with the latest design guidelines for OSX is highly desirable.

Front End Engineer

You write fast, beautiful JavaScript to create simple and powerful user experiences. You translate visual designs into fully working web pages, and edit the HTML, CSS, and Javascript for existing page templates. You build and work with high-performance, reusable code for UI components and your work is always modular and scaleable.

Back End Engineer

You implement robust and scalable systems in distributed environments and enjoy finding simple solutions to complex problems. You have the ability to convert high level design goals into clear and specific implementation plans. You care deeply about the performance of the systems that you build.

Search Engineer

You know search algorithms and have applied them in a commercial setting. You can dive into elaborate datasets and architect the algorithms behind modern search. You are excited to use your mathematical and algorithmic background to help us fix file management.

Data Science Engineer

You are excited by the underlying structure and patterns hidden within complex datasets. You discover valuable metrics and insights from low level user interaction data. You transform abstract ideas into scalable production systems. You have experience leveraging user data to increase the quality of search engines using modern information retrieval techniques.

Jack of All Trades

You may not see the perfect fit in our existing job descriptions, but you love our concept and want to be involved. You are excited about revolutionizing the way people interact with their files. Meta offers challenges in user experience design, marketing, consumer behavior, and nearly every area of software engineering. If you think you would be a good fit, we can’t wait to hear from you!
