All of your files, one place to search

Say goodbye to opening multiple tabs and clicking in and out of different folders to get to a document. With Meta, you’ll be able to access all of your files from a single interface.

Access any file instantly

Meta’s lightning fast search will get you to any file you’re looking for right away, no matter where it’s stored. Meta ranks your search results so the file you’re looking for is always right at the top. Best of all, Meta allows you to open the file in your favorite applications.

Get organized without the hassle

You have hundreds (or thousands) of documents on your computer, and more coming in every day. Meta automatically tags all of these documents for you so you don’t have to take time out of your day to organize.

Automatically personalized for you

Meta learns from your behavior and preferences so your searches and tags get better the more you use Meta, allowing you to spend less time searching and more time getting things done.

Stay focused

Don’t stop what you’re doing to organize. With Meta, the tagging process is seamlessly integrated into your workflow. A couple clicks each time you download, create, or receive a new file will keep you completely organized- and you’ll never have to open or close a window.

Search without the memorization

Don’t remember what you named a file? Or specific content from the file? We’ve got you covered! Meta’s powerful search allows you to search by concepts and topics, in addition to filenames and keywords.
